The distance between us

90

Days of silence

Ninety days without your voice. Without your laugh. Without you. And every single one of those days, I thought of you.

From Divya — with love and honesty

Shrikant, I have been sitting with something heavy on my chest for a long time now. I was rude to you. Not once by accident — but repeatedly, carelessly, in ways that must have stung more than I ever allowed myself to admit. I am sorry. Deeply, truly sorry.

The words I used, the tone I carried, the coldness I showed you — none of it was fair. You were always patient. Always steady. And I repaid that with sharpness when I should have given you softness. That is something I carry every single day.

"I do not just miss you. I miss the person I should have been for you."

These ninety days have changed me. Quietly, slowly — the way a candle changes a room. I have looked at my own behaviour with honest eyes and I did not always like what I saw. But I faced it. Because you deserve someone who is willing to grow, not just someone who is sorry they got caught being unkind.

I want to come back to you — not to the version of us where I was careless with your feelings, but to something warmer, kinder, and more intentional. I know that trust has to be rebuilt slowly. I am not asking for everything at once. I am asking for a beginning.

You are the person I want beside me, Shrikant. Not because I am lonely without you — but because life simply makes more sense with you in it. You bring out something in me that I do not want to lose. And I am ready, finally, to protect that the way it always deserved to be protected.

With all my love and all my honesty —
Divya 🤍

One question

Will you give us another chance?

I am not asking for perfection. I am asking for one warm conversation, one step forward, together.
